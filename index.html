Gingerbread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 g flour
  • 100 g sugar
  • 4 tbsp cocoa powder, heaped
  • 2.5 tbsp gingerbread spice
  • 1 packet baking powder
  • 5 dl milk
  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 4 tbsp liquid honey
  • a little milk (for brushing)

Instructions:

In a large bowl, first thoroughly mix all dry ingredients together. Then add milk, oil, and honey and process into a uniform dough using either a stand mixer or hand mixer.

Spread the finished dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and distribute evenly. Alternatively, the dough can be divided between two greased baking pans, which will make the gingerbread higher and fluffier. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees (Celsius) for about 30 minutes.

Brush the still-warm gingerbread thinly with milk. After cooling completely, cut into uniform square pieces.
